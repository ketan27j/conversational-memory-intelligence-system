## Something to flag for the retrospective

There's a real gap here between "the build loop says this milestone is done" (2026-07-14, L4
VERIFY APPROVE) and "a human actually confirmed understanding of it" (the M1 quiz-me gate wasn't
answered until 2026-07-16 — see that date's entry). Two days sat between an AI verifier's
independent APPROVE and a human's own sign-off. The handbook's own accountability rule (§12) is
exactly about not letting that gap go unnoticed: an L4 VERIFY pass is a second AI's opinion, not a
substitute for the person who owns the submission actually engaging with what got built. Worth
naming directly in `project_retrospective.pdf` as a pattern to watch, not just a fact about M1.
